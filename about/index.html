Welcome

Welcome to my little space. This place collects some random thoughts which I tend to forget if I don’t write them down, notes for my own reference, and some hopefully creative works I did. fermion is an interesting nickname coming from quantum statistical physics. It is a class of microscopic particles that is unique in the sense that no two fermions can have the same quantum numbers. In other words, each fermion is unique. Pretty much like you and me: each of us is special in one way or the other.

What I do for living

I was a theoretical condensed matter physicist. In my research, I used numerical approaches, mostly quantum Monte Carlo methods, to study properties of strongly correlated electron systems. A couple years ago, I made a career change and became a Data Scientist. I like the process of problem solving, so I find it quite rewarding that some business problems can be tackled by means of theoretical physics tools that I am familiar with.

Things I like to do when I’m not working

I’m a flute enthusiast. I have been taking flute lessons since 2014 and am really enjoy playing the most beautiful woodwind instrument and making good music. Typically I practice 1.5 ~ 2 hours a day, 4 days a week in order to stay in shape. My favorite flutists include Peter-Lukas Graf, Emmanuel Pahud, and Denis Bouriakov. I like Baroque and Classical era repertoire, particularly the music written by J. S. Bach and W. A. Mozart. Bach’s flute sonata in E Minor and Mozart’s flute quartet in D Major are my all time favorites.

Because my job requires a lot of screen time, I try to stay fit by jogging and playing badminton. Badminton is a racket sport that I really like since high school. I play mostly men’s doubles. To be really good at it, even only at the advanced recreational level, requires strength, agility, speed, and precision. You also need coordination between you and your partner. So it is quite challenging to be competitive. I once partnered with a friend in a local tournament and made it to semifinal (advanced recreational level of course), a very memorable experience.

Astrophotography. I started doing astrophotography about two years ago. Back when I was still a student, I took interest in astrophotography but could not afford to do it because equipment such as the equatorial mount and telescope are expensive. It is until a few years ago I am able to get into this hobby. Fortunately the seeing in my neighborhood is pretty decent, so I am able to snap pretty nice pictures from my backyard.
